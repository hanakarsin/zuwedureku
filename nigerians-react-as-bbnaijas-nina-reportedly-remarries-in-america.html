Nigerians React As BBNaija's Nina Reportedly Remarries In America

July 28, 2024
Post

Nigerians on social media have reacted following the alleged second marriage of former Big Brother Naija housemate, Nina Ivy.

Naija News reports the Imo State-born TV reality star tied the knot with an African-American, Chris Miller.

According to reports, the couple dated for seven months after they first met in 2021, before finally tying the knot privately in a court in the United States of America.

Following the union, the reality TV star has since changed her name on all social media platforms to the husband’s name, Nina Ivy Miller.
