PDP Extends Sale Of Forms For All Offices

July 09, 2024
Post

Iyorchia Ayu-led National Working Committee of the Peoples Democratic Party on Wednesday extended the sale of nomination forms for all offices from the 1st of April to the 8th of April.

The development was disclosed in a statement released by the National Organizing Secretary of the party, Umar Bature.

The statement was titled, ‘Extension of Sales of Forms and Rescheduling of Screening Exercises’.

According to the main opposition party, the submission of completed Forms Exercises for both the State House of Assembly and National Assembly have been shifted to April 10, 2022.

Their screening has been rescheduled to April 12 and 14, 2022 respectively.

PDP had pegged its nomination and expression of interest forms for presidential aspirants at N40m while “State House of Assembly Expression of Interest: N100,000; Nomination Form, N500,000. House of Representatives: Expression of Interest, N500,000; Nomination Form, ₦2,000,000.

“Senate: Expression of Interest, N500,000; Nomination Form, ₦3,000,000. Governorship: Expression of Interest, ₦1,000,000; Nomination Form, 20,000,000.

“President: Expression of Interest, ₦5,000,000; Nomination Form, N35,000,000.”
