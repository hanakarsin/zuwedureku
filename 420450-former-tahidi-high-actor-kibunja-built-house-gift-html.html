Former Tahidi High Actor Kibunja to be Gifted House by Well-Wisher

June 08, 2024
Post
  • Two weeks since ex-Tahidi High actor Omosh was gifted a house, a fellow cast member is set to benefit from the same
  • This is after revelations by a well-wisher that Kibunja will become a homeowner in the next four months
  • The news comes weeks after fellow actor Omosh was handed the keys to a house given by a corporate entity

On July 14, the country witnessed a new homeowner as former Tahidi actor Peter Kinuthia aka Omosh was officially handed the keys to a new house.

Omosh's house was a gift from SUNG Timber after the thespian went public with the revelation that he was struggling financially and risked being ejected from his house over rent arrears of KSh 100,000.

The house is a three-bedroom master ensuite with an American open plan kitchen and two bathrooms.

Kibunja to be homeowner

The windfall seems to have located another actor from the Tahidi High stable; one Kibunja.

Speaking to Hiram on the Kamuhunjia Show, George Mburu, CEO Mizizi Homes explained that the company is on a mission to build an off-plan house for him.

"Kibunja came to Mizizi as an MC for a company event and he did a good job as much as we didn't pay him a lot. But now we want to appreciate him and since we can only give what we have, and our job is building," he said.
