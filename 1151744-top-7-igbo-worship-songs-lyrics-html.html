Top 7 Igbo worship songs and their lyrics

Post

There are a large number of Christians in Nigeria and amongst them, many are Igbo by tribe. Most of these worshippers often sing several songs in their mother tongue that a lot of people have come to like.

But a lot of people do not know how to properly sing these songs because they do not know the lyrics. As a result of this, Legit.ng has curated a list of seven of the most popular Igbo worship songs.
