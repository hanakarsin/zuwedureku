Michuki Technical Training Institute fee structure, courses, and contacts

March 23, 2024
Post

Michuki Technical Training Institute (MTTI) is a Technical, Vocational, and Entrepreneurial Training (TVET) centre committed to offering high-quality education in a conducive environment. The institution uses technology to empower trainees to be globally competitive and develop professionally. As a public institution under the ministry of education, the technical centre offers courses streamlined to meet the current business environment and job markets. It also allows students to improve individually by enabling them to participate in extracurricular activities.

Michuki Technical Training Institute has some of the best technical education you can find in the country. Courses offered are those that are in demand based on the changes in industries and job markets.

Courses offered at Michuki Technical Training Institute

MTTI offers crafts, artisan, diploma, and certificate courses for all prospects. Some of the courses offered include:

Information Communication Technology Mod 1

The course targets O level individuals that wish to have a career in the Information Technology sector. Graduates can become programmers, database designers and administrators, web developers, and communication specialists.

The program is available in three modules. Module 1 takes three terms while Modules 2 and 3 take two terms each. Program dates begin every January and September.

For ICT Mod 1, the fee per term is KES 9,000. The examining body is KNEC

Electrical Engineering (Power) Mod 1

The course targets individuals who wish to acquire electronics and electrical skills. It prepares students for future employment in the electronics field. At the end of the course, trainees will be able to work accurately, efficiently, and safely in the electronic sector.

MTTI has a diploma program, crafts and artisan certificates in electrical engineering. The duration for a diploma course is two years, and that of a certificate is six terms. The Michuki Technical Training Institute intake for the course is always in January and September.

For a diploma program in Electrical Engineering (Power) Mod 1, the fee per term is KES 9,000. The examining body is KNEC.

Business Management Mod 1

The institution created the course to develop and enhance knowledge and competence in students. It also aims at ensuring students are effective, economical, accountable, and efficient at managing organisational and strategic operations. Those who wish to pursue a career in the world of business can take on the course.

The course duration is modular for a diploma, and the examining body is KNEC. To get a Diploma in Business Management, the fee per term is KES 13,120. The intake is every January and May.

Automotive Engineering Mod 1

The course falls under MTTI’s mechanical engineering department. It targets individuals who aspire to be automotive engineers in the future. It teaches students how to build and design the system and mechanics of cars. Students will learn laboratory works as well as environmental, electrical, and mechanical engineering.

Food and Beverage Mod 1

It is a course created to teach students the basics of cooking, baking, nutrition, and food safety procedures. Admission requirements

Getting admitted at MMIT will depend on the course you are undertaking. Generally, you need the following documents during admission. Original and photocopies of:

  • Result Slips – KCPE, KCSE or Module result slip
  • National ID Card
  • School Leaving Certificate
  • Birth Certificate

You must deliver the documents by hand or send them to the principal using the contact information provided in the section below. It is only after handing the documents that you will receive the Michuki TTI admission letter.

Note that MMIT also admits students using their Mock Results. However, students will only be able to take on the KASNEB Courses. The courses include:

Application

You can download the Michuki Technical Training Institute online application form from the institution’s website. Or, you can visit the same website to complete your application.

Fee structure

The fee payable will depend on the type of program you take. You can find more information about the fee structure on the school’s brochure.

Graduation

The third Michuki Technical Training Institute graduation ceremony happened on 15th March 2019. Students can only graduate if they have undertaken all units in their course and have scored satisfactory grades as per the requirements of the institution.

Where is Michuki Training Institute located?

You will find MTTI in Kangema sub-county in the expansive Muranga County. The institution is about 110 kilometres from Nairobi via Embu-Nairobi highway. It takes about 150 minutes to arrive at the training centre from Nairobi.

If you have any correspondence, you can address it to The Principal, Michuki Technical Training Institute. You can also use any of the following contacts:

  • P.O BOX: 4-10202 Kangema
  • Telephone: 020-3526533 or 0725912313
  • Email: michukitech@yahoo.com
  • Website: michukitech.ac.ke
