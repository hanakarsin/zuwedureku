I Hate Promoting My Music Wizkid (Video)

May 04, 2024
Post

Afrobeats star, Wizkid has revealed that he dislikes music promotion and everything related to press releases.

He stated this in a recently surfaced old video from 2021 where he also bragged about recording some massive hit tracks like Ojuelegba, Holla at Your Boy, and others simply seated comfortably in the studio.

Wizkid explained that he does not see the need to promote his music because he has a good knowledge of what the audience wants and that’s exactly what he gives to them.

The ‘most awarded African artiste’ maintained he’s not cut out for stress but is someone who loves to ‘chill’ (relax, take things easy, and have fun).

He said, “All my biggest songs from Holla at your Boy’ to Soko, Joro, Ojuelegba, I recorded sitting down.

“You know how my thing be for Lagos, you’ve been in my house. You know wagwan, I’m chilling. I’m chilling, man. You get me? No stress.

“You see promo, I hate doing that shit. You know press, I hate doing all that shit bro. So like, I knew exactly what the people wanted, like I understand.”
