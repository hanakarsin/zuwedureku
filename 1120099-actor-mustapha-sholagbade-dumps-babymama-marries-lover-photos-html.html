Actor Mustapha Sholagbade dumps babymama, marries new lover (photos)

May 05, 2024
Post

Popular Yoruba actor Mustapha Sholagbade on Saturday, August 12, got married to his new lover in Oshogbo, Osun state.

Also known as Lollyboy, the movie star has dumped his two babymamas Juwon Quadri and filmmaker/makeup artist, Adewunmi Fatai. The two ladies both gave birth to baby boys the same year.

Lollyboy’s wedding was reported to have been witnessed by friends, colleagues, family members and other well-wishers.

The wedding was said to have been sponsored by a chieftain of the National Union of Road Transport Workers (NURTW), Musiliu Akinsanya popularly known as, MC Oluomo.

The groom also took to his Instagram handle to thank everyone saying: “An eternal thanks to Almighty Allah for all the blessings bestowed on me through my most cherished ones and also from my parents (The Sholagbades and The Olaniyis) to my friends, my god fathers, my spiritual fathers, my bosses, my colleagues, my well-wishers and my fans. I can’t thank you all enough, heaven will blessed y’all for me. I am humbled and grateful. With love Mr&Mrs Sholagbade.”
