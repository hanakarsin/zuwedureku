2023: 'I Will Never Support Tinubu'

February 08, 2024
Post

President Muhammadu Buhari’s Special Adviser on Political Matters, Babafemi Ojudu has revealed what he will do if his principal decides to support Bola Tinubu for presidency.

According to Ojudu, he would go back to farming if Buhari choses the National Leader of the All Progressive Congress as his presidential candidate.

Ojudu stated this during an interview with Chude Jideonwo.

The presidential aide when asked if he would support Tinubu if President Buhari chooses him as his candidate said he would never.

He further stated that he would never support or work with someone he does not believe in.

In his words, ”Never! I’ll never support it. Write it down I’ll go back to the farm.

“If I don’t believe in you, I’ll not work with you or for you.”

Recall that the presidential aide had one time stated that he would not support the presidential ambition of the former Governor of Lagos State.

Ojudu in his stand condemned the belief that anyone who does not support Tinubu is a traitor.

The presidential aide who wished the national leader of the ruling party well in his presidential ambition, added that he would not vote for him.
