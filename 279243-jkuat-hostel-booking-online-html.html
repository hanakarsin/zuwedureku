How to book JKUAT hostels online (on-campus accommodation)

April 14, 2024
Post

The Jomo Kenyatta University of Agriculture and Technology (JKUAT) is a well known public university with quality education to match. It is a public institution of higher learning which offers various courses ranging from medicine to engineering. For those students who wish to join the campus, it is crucial to note that the institution offers sufficient accommodation in the form of internal hostels and a variety of private hostels available outside the campus premises. To qualify for the available internal accommodation, the student must comply with JKUAT hostel booking process and guidelines.

Initially, hostels application JKUAT takes place in the form of online application, whereby the student must access their student portal and place an application on the e-portal, and by manual application, where the student must personally visit the school accommodation offices and fill in some forms. However, the manual hostel booking procedure is not used.

The accommodation process

JKUAT hostel booking before was limited to a manual application process. The students were required to visit the accommodation office and fill in forms in a very time-consuming process. However, the institution was able to integrate an online system of hostel booking where the students must log into their student portal to access the accommodation services online.

The online reservation process follows a very detailed procedure, and all the new and continuing students are expected to follow to acquire a room successfully.

  • For new undergraduate students, the email that is used by the individual in the admission page is the same email that will be used for log in on the portal.
  • Existing JKUAT students are expected to use the official school admission/registration number and their student email to log in to their student portal.
  • To be able to view the JKUAT hostel booking option, the student is expected to log in to their student portal with the required credentials.
  • Once redirected on to the JKUAT hostel booking portal, the student can filter the hostel, room, and bed that they prefer to have within the school grounds; this can be either at the JKUAT Juja campus hostels or the JKUAT Karen campus hostels.
  • The hostel shall ONLY be paid for if the student has already made the reservation and confirmed all the details. There is no special reservation for any payments made before booking. The standard JKUAT hostel prices are six thousand five hundred Kenyan shillings per semester.
  • For students that prefer private hostels, relevant information is also available for download on the site.

JKUAT hostels accommodation groups into two based on gender. Male hostels are not on the same side as female hostels. The hostel's application is on a strict first come first serve basis.

The JKUAT hostels booking for each academic year commences in September

It is a requirement that first-year students log in with:

  • Personal email
  • Registration number

For continuing students, bookings will commences in August.

In order to log in use:

  • Student email
  • Registration number.

Off-campus accommodation

For the students that prefer private hostel accommodation outside the JKUAT campus, it is possible to acquire hostels near JKUAT. These are approved private hostel owners who work in partnership with the campus to ensure that even away from campus grounds; the students are in a safe and secure area. Private hostels offer nearly the same facilities as the school hostels, and it is one of the reasons that the school can collaborate successfully and offer services to the students.

Apart from JKUAT main campus hostels, there are quite some private hostels within the proximity of the school.

1. Baraka Hostels

These student hostels sit about two hundred meters from the JKUAT main campus gate. The compound has a full-time caretaker who is available for viewing at all times. They offer shared rooms of 2 students per room and there is also the option for single rooms. The prices go at 3500 KES for two people sharing a self-contained room and 6500 KES for a single person room inclusive of water and electricity. The hostel is a mixed hostel.

2. Gezer Hostels

Located only 100 meters from the JKUAT main campus gate, these hostels offer accommodation to female students only. The compound is secure, and there is a female caretaker. The living options are four sharing at 3500 KES monthly or 2 sharing at 4000 KES per month, and this is inclusive of water and electricity.

3. Unique Hostel

It is 300 meters from the JKUAT main campus gate. There is a male caretaker available; this is a male only hostel. The two students share a room at 4000 KES per month or 14000 KES each per semester. There is a caretaker available.

4. Immaculate Hostel

100 meters from the JKUAT main campus gate. It is a female only hostel with a female caretaker. The rates are 3500KES per month for four people sharing one self-contained unit.

5. Goshen Hostel

This is a male-only hostel. It is located 300 meters from JKUAT main campus gate B. It has a male caretaker. The rates are 3500KES per month for four males sharing one room. A single goes for 4000KES per month.

6. Safari Hostel

Approximately 300 meters from JKUAT main campus gate B. This is a male only hostel with an available male caretaker. The rooms classify as two people sharing one room for 3500KES per month or 14000KES per semester.

1. Elite Hostels

This hostel is located in off Magadi Road and is in proximity to the Nairobi National Park. The team offers accommodation for both male and female students on separate sides of the compound. The name itself can give you a rough idea of the kind of services offered by the team. The grounds have single rooms that go for 62000 KES per semester.

The double rooms value at 58000 KES per semester and a room of four sharing costs 50000 KES per semester. The hostels also offer transport to and from the grounds daily; there is a high-speed Wi-Fi connection, there is hot water and a constant steady supply of water. They also offer meals for the students, on weekdays they provide breakfast and dinner, during the weekend they provide all the three meals: breakfast, lunch, and dinner. Additionally, they also give 4:00 PM tea. The premises are also in very close proximity to a police post.

2. St. Francis Hostels

It sits along Bogani Road, opposite the Catholic University of East Africa and about twenty five meters from JKUAT Karen campus. It offers a place for ladies and religious sisters. They offer rooms which can accommodate singles at a rate of 35000 per trimester on a full board basis, which means the student receives all the available services such as breakfast and dinner daily, own beddings, water, and electricity.

The shared rooms for two people have a rate of 25000 per person per trimester on a full board basis. The facility offers a laundry area and group facilities such as a TV room and recreational facilities. They also take part in group prayers.

3. Mirvins Hostels

Located near Nairobi Academy opposite Kenol Petrol Station on Mokoyeti-West off Lang’ata Road, Mirvins Hostels offers accommodation to male students only. The hostels are about fifteen minutes walk from JKUAT Karen campus. They have fifty six single rooms that come furnished with a bed, wardrobe, chair and reading table. There is a free Wi-Fi connection, and they have DSTV Cable as well.

They have a modern dining hall with a TV that can change into a conference room also. They also provide a clean and steady supply of water inclusive of instant heating showers. The rates are 59000 Kenyan shillings per student per semester on a full board basis which includes all meals weekdays, weekends and public holidays.

For recreation, they offer pool tables. The team also provides free transport in the morning and evening to and from the compound. There is 24-hours round the clock security. They also provide bedding.

4. St Joseph and Anthony Center

A female only hostel, St Joseph and Anthony Center is situated on Bogani East road and is a walking distance from JKUAT Karen. They have 24-hour security available with guards.

The compound has a 24 hour Wi-Fi connection, and they offer breakfast and dinner over the weekdays and breakfast, lunch, and dinner on the weekends and public holidays. The rates are 17500 Kenyan shillings per semester. They have self-contained rooms and a dining hall along with laundry facilities.
