Chase Hudson - Age, Family, Bio

February 09, 2024
Post

Social media personality and entertainer best known as Huddy on TikTok. He has amassed more than 32 million followers lip-syncing to songs of artists such as Justin Bieber and Pitbull. In 2019, he became a member of the collaborative TikTok group The Hype House alongside Charli D'Amelio, Addison Rae and many other popular TikTok creators. He is a singer and is known for popular original songs such as "21st Century Vampire" and "America's Sweetheart."

Before Fame

Prior to gaining fame on TikTok, he first created a YouTube channel.  

Trivia

He's earned more than 11 million followers on Instagram, where he posts stylish photos of himself, occasionally alongside friends. He was a member of the 2019 Lights Out Summer Tour. He starred in the 2021 film Downfalls High, a film adaptation of the Machine Gun Kelly album Tickets to My Downfall. 

Family Life

He was born in Oakland and raised in Stockton, California to Tamora and Cole Hudson. He grew up alongside his two sisters, Marlena Hudson and Karissa. He was in a relationship with Cynthia Parker in 2019. He then dated Charli D'Amelio from late 2019 to April 2020. 

Associated With

The first two TikTok creators he began collaborating with were Payton Moormeier and Avani Gregg.
