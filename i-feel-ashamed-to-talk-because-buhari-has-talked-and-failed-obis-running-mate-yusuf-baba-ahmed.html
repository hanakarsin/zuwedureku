'I Feel Ashamed To Talk, Because Buhari Has Talked And Failed'

March 13, 2024
Post

The Labour Party (LP) vice presidential candidate, Yusuf Baba-Ahmed has knocked the current administration of President Muhammadu Buhari, over the failure to deliver his campaign promises to Nigerians.

Speaking during an interview on Channels TV, Peter Obi’s running mate said, he and the party’s candidate have the solutions to solve the insecurity challenges in the country but would rather act instead of talking.

According to Yusuf, Nigerians are tired of hearing people talk and do the opposite, the reason he and Peter Obi are completely different from other politicians.

The vice presidential candidate said they are seeking an opportunity to act because the importance of talking has been destroyed by the failure of the current administration.

He added that he feels ashamed to talk because some people like Buhari have talked but failed to deliver the promises.

He said: “We do, the answer is that we have the solution, my good friend take it as that, I like to give you answers but I will like to ask you, what is it that Nigerians have not heard? What did APC not tell you? I don’t want to call names, what did Buhari not say about restoring security?

“What allegations substantiated did they not make? are not Nigerians not tired of hearing people talk and do the opposite? His Excellency Peter Obi and I are a completely different mix of these kinds of politicians.

“That is why we are seeking that opportunity, when we get that opportunity, we would deliver, the importance of talking has been destroyed by the failure of this administration.

“I feel ashamed to talk because someone like Buhari has talked and he failed, so I don’t want to talk, I want to do, I want to act, we have the solution and by God’s grace, Nigeria will be safe and secured, I’m not going to be one of those who talk recklessly, neither will Peter Obi be one of those.”
