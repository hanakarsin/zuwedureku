Top 10 fashion schools in Nigeria

June 15, 2024
Post

It can be said without a doubt that Nigerians have a great sense of style and a desire to look good all the time; this has kept fashion designers in very high demand in the country. If you have a desire to become a fashion designer in Nigeria, then you really should check out this list of the best fashion schools in Nigeria to help you choose the appropriate educational institution for you.

Top 10 best fashion schools in Nigeria

If you intend to become a perfect stylist, then choose one of the 10 schools listed below. You are guaranteed to obtain high-quality theoretical knowledge and practical skills.

Celebrity's Fashion Academy

This educational institution is located in Lagos. Here you can learn how to make sketches, sew children's dresses and clothes for adults. The curriculum contains many interesting special activities.

Nobel Afrik Fashion Training

This school is also located in Lagos. If you have absolutely zero knowledge in the field of design, you should definitely consider this option as they cater for beginners and give them all the necessary information to develop their creative abilities.

TFA

This is a wonderful school and work shop located in Abuja. They have qualified teachers to properly explain the theoretical bases and help students obtain practical skills.

Hats of the Best Fit

This school is worth choosing if you want to focus more on making accessories. You will learn how to make jewelry, headgear and much more. The educational institution is located in Lagos.

Addurrah Creations

This school located in Lagos is a good option for people who are yet to discover what kind of design they want to specialize in. They will teach you about tailoring, making accessories and other types of design art.

Fashion and Art Academy

Professors of this educational institution in Lagos teach a general theory of design and related disciplines. The school is considered the best in Africa.

Valisimo Fashion School in Lagos

Here you will learn to sew evening and everyday outfits for women, men and children.

KG School of Fashion in Lagos

The teachers here will show you how to make patterns and sew beautiful clothes. It also provides you with the convenient option of studying online.

Cheryl Cold Fashion School

This is another place in Lagos where you can get general knowledge on fashion and design.

Ginani Fashion School

This last school on our list is also in Lagos. Here you will be taught to design clothes, create beautiful patterns and much more.

As you can see, most fashion schools on our list are located in Lagos. This is due to the fact that this city is one of the most developed in Nigeria and Africa in general. Every Nigerian has the opportunity to become a good designer, achieve fame and high wages if they get proper training.

Types of designers

Having decided to venture into the world of fashion designing, you need to understand what kind of fashion designers you want to become. There are several kinds of fashion designers, depending on the things they work with:

  • Clothing. You can choose a certain direction (attires for men, women or children). Decide what interests you more between casual outfits, evening costumes and dresses, sporting outfits, coats and jackets or maybe even underwears.
  • Footwear. Nigerians appreciate beautiful shoes and sneakers no less than clothes. If you can come up with original ideas and designs made with unique materials, your work is guaranteed to be popular.
  • Accessories. No outfit can be considered complete without accessories. Do not underestimate the importance of items like bags, scarves, bracelets, glasses, beads, hats, etc. Sometimes they are created by clothing designers as part of a general image.
  • Festive/stage costumes. If you have a lot of interesting ideas related to attires for television shows, theatrical performances and movies, then you should consider going into this branch of fashion designing. This is a job for designers with good creative abilities.

These categories listed above are the most common ones. However, you do not need to choose only one. Your profession can be mixed. Learn about your abilities and passions to understand what kind of jobs can bring the best results.
