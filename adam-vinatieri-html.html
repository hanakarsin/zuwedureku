Adam Vinatieri - Age, Family, Bio

July 20, 2024
Post

NFL placekicker known for his game-winning field goals with the Colts and the Patriots. He won three Super Bowl titles with the Patriots and one title with the Colts. He has held the record for most postseason points scored and most overtime field goals made.

Before Fame

He played one season with the NFL Europe Amsterdam Admirals after playing college football for South Dakota State University.

Trivia

He was named to the NFL 2000s All Decades Team as well as the Patriots All-1990s and All-2000s teams.

Family Life

He has two children, AJ and Allison, with his wife Valeri Vinatieri, whom he married in 2001.

Associated With

He was teammates with Tom Brady on three Super Bowl-winning teams.
