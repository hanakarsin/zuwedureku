Samuel Eto'o Elected Cameroon FA President

Post
Former Inter Milan and Barcelona striker Samuel Eto’o has been elected president of the Cameroon Football Federation (Fecafoot). Naija News reports that Eto’o, who polled 43 votes, defeated the incumbent president, Seidou Mbombo Njoya, who scored 31 votes. Eto’o was acting as the global ambassador for the Qatar 2022 World Cup while Njoya is currently the fourth vice-president of the Confederation of African Football. Seven candidates had planned to contest the elections, but five of them pulled out on the morning of the elections.
