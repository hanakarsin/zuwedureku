It is not easy to build a sturdy democracy. Even in long-established ones, democracy can corrode if not nurtured and protected. The democracy index measures the state of democracy in 167 countries worldwide. The index is based on 60 indicators grouped in five different categories: - electoral process and pluralism, - civil liberties, - functioning of government, - political participation, - political culture. In addition to a numeric score and a ranking, the index categorizes countries as on of four regime types full democracies, flawed democracies, hybrid regimes, and authoritarian regimes.
