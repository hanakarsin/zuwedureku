In a related article published on TUKO.co.ke, Pastor Burale asked Kenyans to stop criticising celebrities who marry spouses younger or older than them.

He said that as long as somebody is of legal age, they shouldn't be trolled because of who they choose to settle down with.

"When two grown-ups decide to get married, nobody should abuse them or mock them or bully them because of their age difference. They have decided to come together, it is their happiness and decision. It is sad when people interfere with other people's marriages because of age differences. It's something that really needs to stop," Burale said.
